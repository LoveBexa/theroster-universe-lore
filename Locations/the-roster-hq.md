# The Roster HQ

Six floors of a glass tower over the Thames — The Roster's headquarters in its early, idealistic days.

## Details

- Reception smelled faintly of cedar and coffee; living moss walls climbed toward the ceiling.
- Meeting rooms were named after human emotions: Trust, Wonder, Joy, Belonging.
- Employees wore cream-coloured clothing — not a uniform, but a policy based on internal studies claiming neutral colours reduced workplace anxiety by 11%.
- Sub-Level Seven, hidden beneath the building, houses [[Locations/the-archive|The Archive]].

## Notable residents

- [[Characters/mia|Mia]] worked here in Behavioural Systems.

## History

- Home base during The Roster's transition from a relationship-companion app to the portfolio-style dashboard product described in [[Projects/roster-league|The Roster League]].
