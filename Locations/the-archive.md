# The Archive

A hidden sub-level beneath [[Locations/the-roster-hq|The Roster HQ]] where surrendered sentimental objects go to be forgotten.

## Details

- No signs, no maps — only the word "ARCHIVE" etched into the concrete.
- Thousands of small white boxes stretching into darkness, each holding physical objects users surrendered during "account optimisation": engagement rings, birthday cards, Polaroids, train tickets, letters, pressed flowers, a child's drawing.
- These are objects with no measurable value — things the algorithms couldn't quantify.
- An employee told Mia the objects are kept out of sight because when people believe one relationship matters more than every prediction, they stop generating useful data.

## History

- [[Characters/mia|Mia]] found the Archive by accident and read a letter with no user ID or optimisation score: *"I know the statistics say this won't last. I don't care. I choose you anyway."* It was the first thing she'd seen in months that wasn't trying to become data.
