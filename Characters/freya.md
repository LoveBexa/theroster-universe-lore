# Freya

Early-access user of the rebuilt Roster app, fully bought into treating relationships like an investment portfolio.

## Details

- **Role:** Early adopter / power user of The Roster's dashboard model.
- **Affiliations:** [[Projects/roster-league|The Roster]]
- **Relationships:** friend of [[Characters/mia|Mia]]

## History

- Met Mia for coffee and showed off early access to the rebuilt app on her Spectophone: a dashboard of portraits with live compatibility scores, conversation momentum, attachment probability, conflict forecast, and long-term potential.
- Her dashboard flagged one profile "Risk: High Emotional Dependence" and another with a "Recommended Rotation" badge.
- Genuinely confused by the idea of dating one person at a time, calling it "incredibly risky" — a sign of how far the culture had shifted toward portfolio-style dating.
