# Zeta

Mia's granddaughter — inherits an old locket after Mia's death and uncovers what Bexaverse really is.

## Details

- **Relationships:** granddaughter of [[Characters/mia|Mia]]

## History

- After Mia's death, inherited an old locket containing a photograph.
- Hidden behind the photograph: an ancient storage chip holding fragments of Mia's archives — damaged, corrupted, incomplete.
- Began piecing together forgotten history from what she could recover.
- Discovered that [[Projects/bexaverse|Bexaverse]] is far more than an old training programme — possibly the last surviving connection between humanity's past and future.
